Web Development Process

Your Goals

Your website is unique. Before I write one line of code, I want to gain an understanding of the goals your website is intended to accomplish. I will discover your needs, brand, personality, and company identity. Then, you and I will determine the best next steps for you!

Creating a Plan

After our initial consultation, I will provide you with a proposal that outline the site I will build that reaches your specific needs and goals. We will also finalize specific terms such as payment and timelines!

Wireframes + Mockups

I will create a draft of your website so you can begin to see your vision come to life! I will build everything with your needs, personality, and vision mind!

Web Development

After you approve a final draft, I will start coding your site.

Quality Assurance

Before I launch your new site, I want to double-check all of the technical details that can prohibit a website's success.

Website Launch

Your website is launched, and you are live!

Follow Up
