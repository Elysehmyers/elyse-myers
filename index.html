About Me

My name is Elyse Myers, and I am a self-taught front-end web developer! I love all things coding, coffee, and sloths. I have an immense admiration for the culture, process, and craft of web development. As someone with an artisic background, I am passionate about both the visual and technical aspects of creating a website. I feel incredibly fortunate to be able to communicate effectively across creative and tech teams alike. I care deeply about creating intentional, useful, and beautiful products that help you, and make a difference in your success.

My web-developer skills are growing and evolving daily — If I run into a project that requires skills I don't yet have in my tool belt, I will pour every minute of my time into learning them for you. I am a problem solver at heart, and I pride myself on my meticulous eye for detail (which definitely comes in handy as a web developer!) My mission is to help bring more beauty & creativity into the world by etching out a corner of the interwebs for you to share your great ideas and businesses with the world!
