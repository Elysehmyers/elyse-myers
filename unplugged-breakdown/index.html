Unplugged Retreat

This project was a concept design 'UNPLUGGED: A Tech Industry Retreat.' My goal was to code a responsive website for the retreat which targeted companies and professionals in the tech industry. The Unplugged retreat encourages indivuduals and teams to unplug from technology and spend time in nature.

Taking a mini-retreat from the cubicle life is important for everyone from time to time. By relaxing and bonding in the wilderness, the hope is that those who attend can return to work feeling refreshed, rejuvinated, and with new friendships that will last for years to come!

A web designer had already designed a gorgeous site for me. My role in this project was to translate their design comp into code, making them responsive uing flexbox. I utilized photoshop to extract the designer's fonts, spacing, colors, margins, padding, and images for this site to ensure my code perfecly matched the photoshop designs. This is a three-page site that containted a Homepage, a FAQ page, and an About page. I enjoyed working on a projet with more than one page, because it allowed me to refine my HTML organization skills within a project.

I found myself renaming sections of content multiple times as I wrote my code, and learning quickly that ID's and Class names are essential to efficiency of a project. Overall, I felt very proud of the result of this site. I enjoyed writing code that brought such a beautifully designed site to life. Each section of this site gave me challenges that grew my skills and gave me new ones. I was able to successfully replicate the designs given to me into a responsive website that cleanly translates across mobile phones, tablets, and desktop screens.
